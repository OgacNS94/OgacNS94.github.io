About Me

I am a PhD student at School of Computer and Communication Sciences, EPFL. I am working at the Parallel Systems Architecture Laboratory PARSA with Dr. Mirjana Stojilović. My main area of research is security of remote FPGAs. I have a Computer Science Masters degree from the Faculty of Science and Engineering of the Sorbonne Université, for which I recieved a french government scholarship for international students. I obtained my bachelor studies in Electronics as Elecrical Engineering from the University of Novi Sad. During my sudies, I had multiple industry internships, the most notable one was at ARM France SAS in 2018, and in Elsys Eastern Europe in 2016. In 2018, I joined EPFL as an CS doctoral fellowship student. You can find the most recent version of my CV here.
